How to Run a Fantasy Draft Lottery

January 10, 2025 by FDL Team

Why Your Draft Order Matters

In most fantasy leagues, the draft is the single biggest factor in determining your season. Draft order determines when you pick, which players are available, and what strategy you need to run. Getting the process right before the draft starts sets the tone for the whole league.

The fairest way to set draft order is through a lottery. Whether you use a fully random draw or a weighted system depends on your league's preferences.

Random Draft Order

In a random lottery, every team has an equal chance of landing any pick position. This is the simplest approach and works well for new leagues or leagues where everyone is starting fresh each season.

A random draw removes any politics from the process. No one can argue that the results were influenced. You run the lottery, the order is set, and the draft begins.

Weighted Draft Lottery

A weighted lottery gives teams with worse recent records a better chance at picking earlier. This mirrors how the NBA draft lottery works: teams that performed worse during the season receive more lottery balls, increasing their odds of landing the first overall pick.

For example, in a 10-team league, the last-place team from the prior season might get 10 lottery balls while the first-place team gets 1. Every ball goes into the pool and the draw determines pick order. The more balls a team has, the higher their odds, but it is still a lottery and nothing is guaranteed.

Weighted lotteries work well for leagues that have been running for multiple years and want to build in a competitive balance mechanism.

Tips for Making Draft Day Feel Official

Do It Live

Run the lottery live with your league on a video call or in person. Watching the results reveal one at a time, especially with the draft reveal music playing, adds genuine excitement. Managers react in real time and the anticipation builds throughout the reveal.

Use a Screen Share

If your league is remote, share your screen during the reveal. One commissioner controls the results page and everyone watches together. The one-by-one reveal format is built specifically for this experience.

Record the Reveal

Screen record the reveal and share it in your league chat afterward. It becomes a reference point throughout the season and a piece of league history.

Common Questions About Fantasy Draft Lotteries

How do you determine fantasy draft order?

The most common methods are a random lottery (every team has an equal chance) or a weighted lottery (teams with worse records get more lottery balls and a better chance at top picks). You can run either type for free at fantasydraftlottery.com.

What is a weighted fantasy draft lottery?

A weighted draft lottery assigns different numbers of lottery balls to each team. Teams with more balls have a higher probability of landing an earlier pick. This is similar to the NBA draft lottery, where worse-performing teams get better odds at the top pick.

What is the best free fantasy draft order generator?

Fantasy Draft Lottery is a free, simple draft order generator that supports both random and weighted lotteries for 2 to 32 teams. No signup required and results are revealed pick by pick with optional music.

How many teams can a fantasy draft lottery support?

Fantasy Draft Lottery supports leagues with 2 to 32 teams. Most fantasy leagues run 8 to 14 teams, which falls well within the supported range.
