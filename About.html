About Fantasy Draft Lottery

The Fantasy Season. You love it, you hate it. You want it to last forever, you want it to end immediately.

Fantasy sports is a culture, and for some, a lifestyle. Your league may be an eight team one season football office league, or it may be a 12 team dynasty basketball league amongst life long friends. Either way, the most important part of any league is the draft.

Use Fantasy Draft Lottery to determine your next draft order. Our tool supports random and weighted lotteries for NFL, NBA, MLB, NHL, and any other fantasy sports league with 2 to 32 teams.
